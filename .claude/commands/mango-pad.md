Navigate to the Mango Pad App folder and provide context about it.

The Mango Pad App is located at: `apps/mango-pad`

This is the tablet-optimized application for Mango, designed for:
- Service providers to manage their appointments
- Mobile checkout
- Client notes and history

To launch: `pnpm run dev` from the mango-pad directory.
