Navigate to the Online Store App folder and provide context about it.

The Online Store App is located at: `apps/online-store`

This is the customer-facing online booking and e-commerce application for Mango. It includes:
- Online appointment booking
- Service catalog browsing
- Gift card purchases
- Client account management

To launch: `pnpm run dev` from the online-store directory.
